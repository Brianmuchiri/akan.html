AKAN NAMES

Akan names are derived from Ghanian Culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as 'day name'which corresponds to the day on the week they were born. Most people do not know their birthdays are, but some do not know what day of the week they were born.luckily for us, there are ways to calculate the day of the week from a specific date . Please write the century(CC),year(YY),month(MM,date(DD) to get your akan name.
